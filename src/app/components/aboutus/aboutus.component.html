About Us

EmileAge's mission is to work towards serving the society and ensuring the imminent generations a clean,green and sustainable future.Here at EmileAge we are building Rural and Urban Economical Mobility Systems(RUEMS) for people of various sections from various dimensions to commute between source and destinations. We want to uplift the people from Rural and Urban regions by creating a new way of life by easing the challenges with innovative and efficient commute systems leaving Zero to No-carbon footprints. We at EmileAge develop Micro Mobility Vehicle systems,an user can ride and track the vehicles through an user friendly app,this helps in point to point tracking with precision. Systems developed can be customized to have either have fixed or swappable battery technology
